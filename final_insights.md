# 📊 Sales Data Analysis — Executive Summary

This project analyzes sales, profit, discounts, and returns to identify key business insights and improvement opportunities.

### Key Highlights:
- Total Sales driven by Technology and Office Supplies
- Significant profit leakage in Furniture category
- Discounts above 20% lead to consistent losses
- Central region underperforms due to high discounting
- West region shows unusually high return rates (~15%)
- Several top-selling products are loss-making

The analysis provides actionable recommendations to improve profitability and operational efficiency.

## 💸 Profitability Insights

- Furniture category generates high revenue but very low profit
- Tables sub-category is the biggest loss driver (~ -17K)
- Bookcases and Supplies also contribute to losses
## 📉 Discount Impact

- Discounts above 20% result in negative average profit
- Maximum losses occur in the 40–60% discount range
- High discounts do not significantly increase sales volume

👉 Conclusion: Current discount strategy is unsustainable
## 🌍 Regional Insights

- West region is the most profitable (~108K)
- Central region underperforms despite decent sales
- Central region has the highest average discount (~24%)

👉 High discounting is reducing profitability in Central region
## 🔁 Returns Insights

- Overall return rate is ~8%
- West region has the highest return rate (~15%)
- Return rates are consistent across categories

👉 Returns are region-specific, not product-specific
## 🏆 Product Insights

- Top revenue products are high-value technology items
- Canon Copier is the highest revenue and profit generator

⚠️ Some high-selling products are loss-making:
- Cisco TelePresence
- GBC Binding Systems

👉 High sales does not guarantee profitability
# 💡 Business Recommendations

### 1. Optimize Discount Strategy
- Cap discounts at 20%
- Avoid heavy discounting in Furniture category

### 2. Fix Loss-Making Products
- Re-evaluate pricing for Cisco and GBC products
- Consider discontinuing consistently loss-making items

### 3. Improve Central Region Performance
- Reduce excessive discounting
- Analyze operational inefficiencies

### 4. Investigate High Returns in West Region
- Review logistics and delivery quality
- Analyze customer feedback and product expectations

### 5. Focus on High-Margin Products
- Promote Canon Copier and HP products
- Increase inventory for high-profit items
# 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Return Rate (%)
- Average Discount
- Sales by Category
- Profit by Region
- Top 10 Products
- Loss-Making Products


# 📌 Conclusion

This analysis reveals that profitability is significantly impacted by discounting strategies and regional inefficiencies.

By optimizing discounts, focusing on high-margin products, and addressing return issues, the business can substantially improve its overall performance and profitability.
